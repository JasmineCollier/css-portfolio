About Me

Covid has truly changed the game, I find myself all of a sudden chasing an entirely new career path. My career was in marketing but after a lot of soul searching and the threat of a lay off I decided to switch gears. Looking into coding bootcamps I discovered UX/UI and realized this was exactly what  I was looking for. I studied graphic design in undergrad but gave up on it entirely after heartbreaking comments from a professor.  As an adult I will never let anyone get in the way of my dreams or tell me my own capabilities, not even myself. I am committed to being a UX sponge, forever learning and evolving to always put users first and successfully manage all projects from beginning to end.
